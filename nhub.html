Internship

We offer internship opportunities for undergraduates seeking the learn while working real-world projects. We give undergraduates the chance to serve for 4, 6 months, and one year depending on their school Calendar. They get the chance to learn in the following fields:

  1. Frontend Web Development
  2. Backend web development
  3. Product (UI/UX) Design
  4. Data Analysis
  5. Embedded systems and IoT
  6. Mobile App Development

Placeher

The placeher program is women exclusive program aimed at encouraging more women to join the tech ecosystem. We give them the chance to learn not just tech but even entrepreneurship and more. They get the chance to develop in a career in the following fields:

  1. Frontend Web Development
  2. Backend web development
  3. Product (UI/UX) Design
  4. Data Analysis
  5. Embedded systems and IoT
  6. Mobile App Development

Upskill

This is a business development training for women to equip them with the skills they need to grow and sustain a business. They get the chance to be trained in:

  1. Business Model Canvas
  2. Technology for Business
  3. Crowdfunding Canvas (Alternative to Business financing)
  4. Digital Marketing
  5. Business formalisation
